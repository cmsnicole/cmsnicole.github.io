Promotional Videos

York Conference 2023

The 2023 York Conference is a flagship event of YORKHKPASS that discusses various Hong Kong social issues, such as the restriction of Sai Yeung Choi Street South's pedestrianisation, the launch of the Lantau Tomorrow Vision project and more. In the event, participants would have to play the roles of different stakeholders to balance one another's preferences and find win-win solutions to these issues.

Besides students of the University of York, students from other universities are also welcomed. Hence, I produced this promotional video to attract more participants and give them a glance at the event's content. As a result, the promotional video successfully attracted more than 30 participants, leading to the huge success of the event.
