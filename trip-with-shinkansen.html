Trip with Shinkansen

An app prototype for iPads to raise National Railway Museum(NRM) visitors' interest in the Shinkansen

Project Overview

What is Trip with Shinkansen?

Trip to Shinkansen is an app prototype for iPads targeted to all visitors, especially young children, and families of the National Railway Museum (NRM).

The app aims to raise visitors' interest in the Shinkansen, with the guide of the cute and friendly character Mr. Shinkansen on their way to Hakata, by providing easy-to-understand information and active tasks through exploration of the Shinkansen vehicle's inside area and discovery of its fun facts through a card flip matching game.

What's the problem?

In the past, the NRM has struggled to give visitors a real sense of what it was like to travel in these vehicles from the past.

Hence, the museum is currently looking for solutions to make the historic experience of the Shinkansen, which is no longer able to physically access the vehicle itself, accessible to the visiting public.

Design Approach

  • Desk-based research
  • Usability test

Further issues observed from desk-based research

  • Young children and family groups visitors have the lowest interest in rails.
  • Restricted access to the inside of trains significantly reduces visitors' satisfaction with their visits.
  • The rails' information is delivered with low efficiency.

What's the solution?

Based on the previously identified problems, two main design requirements have been set:

  • To include an inside view of the Shinkansen
  • To ensure that displayed information is easy to understand, and is conveyed in a combination of visuals and text through delivering interactive experiences
