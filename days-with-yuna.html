Days with Yuna

A time management app to provide a new solution for building better habits with in-story companion

Project Overview

What is Days with Yuna?

Days with Yuna is a time management app for encouraging users on building habits of doing chores, and/or other kinds of events. Users can interact with the character Yuna by completing tasks and unlocking stories.

The app aims to let users get rid of their phones and build a habit of doing chores (and/or other habits). It also aims to provide the incentive for users to complete their tasks by unlocking stories of Yuna.

What's the problem?

Young adults in the digital age may become distracted and attached to their smartphones, leading them to trivialize or avoid daily tasks such as chores. This lack of familiarity with efficient chore methods can create a vicious cycle, further reducing their willingness to take the first step and seek help.

What's the solution?

Design requirements are determined by previously mentioned findings.

Functional requirements

Functions to be included:

  • Reminder
  • Pop-up window to lock the phone
  • Timer (full screen & in notifications)
  • Tracker
  • Edit chores to do
  • 'Do it later'
  • whitelist to enable access of chosen apps for emergency / background usage

Non-functional requirements

The app should:

  • Be motivative
  • Be easy to use
  • Let users develop an emotional connection with Yuna
