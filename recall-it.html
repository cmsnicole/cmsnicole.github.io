Recall It!

A 3D walking simulator where the player, as a bear Elise, explores her small house to uncover what she was doing before the start of the story.

Project Overview

What is Recall It?

Recall It! is a 3D walking simulator where players become Elise, a bear in a world of bears who has to recall a forgotten task by walking around her small house and interact with her surroundings.

Ideation of the game

Inspired by my personal experience of forgetting tasks when interrupted, I created Recall It! to recreate this situation in a cute and interesting way, with an added challenge of requiring reasoning and problem-solving skills. The game provides an element of distraction and anxiety relief, making it enjoyable for all audiences.

Main visual design requirements

  • Should use warm lighting to create a cosy atmosphere
  • Should use first-person perspective to make the game more immersive for players to play the role as Elise

Camera and audio design

<

>

  • The placement of the FPS controller and the direction of the camera are intentionally designed to draw the player's attention to important objects and areas, such as the washing machine.
  • The sound design is used to provide subtle hints to the player, such as the sound of the washing machine, which encourages them to explore the game and discover the full story.
  • The use of 3D spatial blending for the washing machine audio ensures that it is only audible when the player is in the kitchen, creating a more immersive experience.
  • The audio design in Recall It! is carefully integrated with the level design to create a seamless and immersive experience for the player.
  • The first task in the game, turning on the speaker, not only introduces the player to the game mechanics, but also sets the tone for the audio design throughout the rest of the game.
