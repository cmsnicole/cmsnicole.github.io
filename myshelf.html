MyShelf

A study-aid website that customises the user experience to provide accessible and unique methods for productivity

Project Overview

What is MyShelf?

MyShelf is an interactive study website designed to block out external distractions and utilise various focus techniques to keep them on task.

The website aims to help students focus and study by using different study methods, while ensuring an accessible and highly customisable experience for users.

My Team Contributions

  • Took the role as the lead designer
  • Created design guidelines for both functional and aesthetic aspects of the website
  • Ensured design guidelines were followed throughout the website's development

What's the problem?

'MyShelf' is primarily influenced by the application 'Forest'. By analysing study-aid applications, it is noticed that there was no explicit representation of an existing application that combines different methods of organisation and study.

However, by diversifying the user experience across many study methods and accessible features, many core fundamental issues found in case studies can be solved.
